LAKE BUNYONYI

July 18 2017

Lake Bunyonyi is located in Uganda between Kisoro and Babale while bordering Rwanda. It was one of the beautiful places I visited while traveling through East Africa! The lake surrounds many islands that inhabit many peoples and even animals. The islands also have churches, schools, and resorts that are open to visitors. While at the lake, I was able to visit schools, villages, and enjoy lake activities. Overall, my trip included teaching and visiting at various schools, interacting with business leaders, learning about the culture, and meeting beautiful individuals!
